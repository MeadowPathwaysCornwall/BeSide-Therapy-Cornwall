Welcome

BeSide Therapy Cornwall offers gentle, person‑centred therapeutic support for children, young people, and adults. Whether through talking, play, creativity, or quiet reflection, every session is shaped around the individual — their needs, their pace, their story.

We welcome referrals from:
Individuals • Parents & carers • Schools • Health & social care professionals • Community organisations

Our Therapeutic Approach

  • Compassion — meeting each person exactly where they are
  • Nature‑inspired calm — grounding, soothing spaces
  • Pace and presence — walking beside, never ahead
  • Creativity and curiosity — because healing looks different for everyone

What to Expect

Beginning therapy can feel daunting, especially if you’re unsure where to start. My role is to walk beside you — never ahead — and to create a space where you can explore what feels important at your own pace.

  • A warm, grounded welcome
  • A calm, non‑judgemental space
  • Time to settle, breathe, and arrive
  • Support shaped around your needs and comfort
  • No pressure to talk before you’re ready

A Gentle Invitation

If you feel I might be the right person to walk beside you or someone you care for, you’re welcome to reach out when you feel ready.
